How It Works

📐

Accurate Geometry

All rafter lengths and angles are calculated from first principles using trigonometry — including hip pitch, backing bevel, and jack-rafter side cuts.

🧱

Insulated Pods

The calculator designs OSB cassette pods containing 100 mm PIR insulation plus a 50 mm ventilated air gap, for a warm, condensation-free roof.

🪵

Optimised OSB Cutting

A bin-packing algorithm minimises waste when cutting pods from standard 2440 × 1220 mm sheets, giving you a sheet-by-sheet cutting schedule.

📄

PDF Manual

Download a complete A4 instruction manual with annotated diagrams, step-by-step assembly phases, cut angles, and a final inspection checklist.
